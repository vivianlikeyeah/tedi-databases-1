Activity:

Name one mailing list that you have subscribed to at some point in your life? Have you ever unsubscribed from a mailing list? Usually, customers emails are stored in a database. When marketing emails are sent out, or customer notifications, everyone on the mailing list (database) is sent the same email. When you select unsubscribe, you are essentially asking for your record (your email) in the database (mailing list) to be deleted.

Databases can do more than store email addresses - almost all data analytics is performed on data that is stored in a database. This includes everything from predicting the weather to determining customer behaviour and market trends. Databases are used to understand genes that cause disease and are used to track football statistics to determine best game-plays. Databases have data added, scraped, and analysed thousands of times every day.
